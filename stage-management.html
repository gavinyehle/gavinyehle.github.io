◊ About ◊

Gavin Yehle is a junior pursuing his BFA in the Stage Management Program at the University of Utah.  He started stage managing for the University of Utah Youth Theatre in high school after being unable to perform in a show, and has since stage managed numerous shows for them as well as at the University of Utah.

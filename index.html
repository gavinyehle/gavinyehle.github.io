◊ About ◊

Gavin Yehle is a senior pursuing his BFA in the Actor Training and Stage Management Programs at the University of Utah.  He has been acting since he was 9 years old largely with the University of Utah Youth Theatre where he was a member of the Youth Theatre Conservatory for six years, which is where he got a start with stage management and design. He has worked as an actor, stage manager, sound designer, mix engineer, assistant lighting designer, dresser, spot operator, and most recently just started his own podcast.

‹›   About me   ‹›

 

Gavin is a sophomore in the Actor Training Program at the University of Utah and has been involved in theatre since he was nine years old, when I was privileged enough to have the opportunity to be  in a play directed by Xan Johnson, a professor at the University of Utah.  This opportunity lead him to become a University of Utah Youth Theatre (UUYT) student.  He was a member of the UUTY conservatory for six years, including being in a show that went to Edinburgh's Festival Fringe.  Within the last few years, he has been able to help with designing of the UUYT mainstage shows, and more recently asked to stage manage for some of these shows.  As a way to give back to this wonderful organization, he started Gavin's Pâtisserie, selling homemade gourmet cupcakes as a fundraiser.  These cupcakes, along with many of my other culinary creations are featured alongside his theatre portfolio on this website.  Gavin is also an avid musician, playing saxophone, bassoon, and, more recently, clarinet.

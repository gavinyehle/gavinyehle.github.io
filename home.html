◊   About me   ◊

 

I am a sophomore in the Actor Training Program at the University of Utah and have been involved in theatre since I was nine years old, when I was privileged enough to have the opportunity to be  in a play directed by Xan Johnson, a professor at the University of Utah.  This opportunity lead me to become a University of Utah Youth Theatre (UUYT) student.  I was a member of the UUTY conservatory for six years, including being in a show that went to Edinburgh's Festival Fringe.  Within the last few years, I have been able to help with designing of the UUYT mainstage shows, and more recently asked to stage manage for some of these shows.  I am also an avid musician, playing saxophone, bassoon, and, more recently, clarinet.

◊ About ◊

Gavin Yehle is a senior pursuing his BFA in the Acting and Stage Management Programs at the University of Utah. He has always loved music, and has played saxophone and bassoon for over ten years. He fell into sound design after taking a few sound classes at the University of Utah, and has been doing it since.
